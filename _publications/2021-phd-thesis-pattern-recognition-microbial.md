This doctoral thesis develops pattern recognition techniques for complex heterogeneous time-series data, with a primary focus on microbial community dynamics analysis. The work addresses the challenges of extracting meaningful patterns from multi-dimensional biological time-series data and proposes novel approaches for understanding microbial community behaviour.

### Recommended citation:
