We present CoPR (Collective Pattern Recognition), a framework for analysing microbial community activity by recognising collective patterns in complex microbiome data. This framework extends our previous work on microbial community dynamics analysis, providing a structured approach to understanding collective behaviours in microbial ecosystems.
